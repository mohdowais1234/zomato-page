Zomato, owned by Eternal Limited, is an Indian multinational that began as a restaurant discovery and directory platform in 2008 and has since expanded into a comprehensive food-tech ecosystem. It offers online food ordering and delivery, restaurant information and reviews, table reservations, and is expanding into other lifestyle services. Founded by Deepinder Goyal and Pankaj Chaddah, Zomato connects millions of customers with restaurants and delivery partners through its platform
